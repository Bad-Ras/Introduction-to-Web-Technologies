Our Story

In 1984 our founder, Enzo Hamilton, had a vision of providing 1st class automotive care. Bringing to Jamaica

professionals with elite qualifications and expertise.This wasn't enough for Enzo, he went even further, obtaining

the highest quality parts and accessories. Searching the globe for suppliers of the best genuine and non-genuine parts.

With this mindset and goal in mind, we here at CLR continue to seek out and provide only the best in parts,

accessories and mechanic services the world over.
